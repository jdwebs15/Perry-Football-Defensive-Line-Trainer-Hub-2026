Game 9 — UNDER Hybrid Trainer

This game combines formation strength, RIP/LIZ calls, UNDER alignment, and post-snap gap responsibility. Offense is on top. Defense is on bottom. Calls are made from the defensive perspective.

RIP / LIZ rule: RIP means the Nose and End go to the defensive right. Tackle and Jack go opposite. LIZ means the Nose and End go to the defensive left. Two detached receivers set the formation strength.

Rules

  • UNDER only: End, Nose, Tackle, Jack.
  • Offense is shown across from the defense: offense on top, defense on bottom.
  • RIP/LIZ is called to the offensive formation strength.
  • Two detached receivers make that side strong.
  • Drag the front when asked. Click the final responsibility when asked.
  • Misses count. Perfect run earns the ticket.
