Start the Drill

This game trains the down block reaction exactly how it has to happen on Friday night: eyes see down, body moves with the down, then react to puller or no puller.

With Puller

Keep moving with the down, shoulder at 45 degrees to the LOS, throw the wrong arm and back hip through the kick out.

Close the Gap

Close the gap the scrape linebacker is vacating. Do not run past the gap and reopen it.

Without Puller

Snap shoulders back parallel to the LOS, eyes to the backfield, and maintain outside leverage on the QB read.
