This teaches OKIE with the alignment rules we use: Nose shades to the call, End is in a 5 technique to the call, Tackle is in a 4i away from the call, and Jack is 4x4 off the ball away from the call.

Rule built into this version: base/man-on, down with no puller, and down with puller are treated as separate reads. Base/man-on maintains the player’s presnap alignment gap. Only on-the-LOS personnel spill pullers. Whoever the puller is heading to boxes if he is off the ball. In OKIE, Sam and Jack are off the ball; if the puller goes to Sam, Sam boxes and Jack holds C away from the call.
