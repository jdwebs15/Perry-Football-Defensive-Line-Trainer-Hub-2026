Start the Drill

This game trains disciplined pass rush. Once you recognize High Hat, you engage fully in pass rush, but you still rush with responsibility. Outside rushers are contain rush always. Inside rushers work the gap-side shoulder of the QB and never cross his face.

High Hat

Recognize the pass set and immediately engage fully in pass rush.

Rush Through YOUR Gap

As the hat and numbers of your key come at you, shoot your hands inside and tight just like any base block.

QB Depth Rule

Never go past the depth of the Quarterback. Squeeze and constrict his area of operation.
