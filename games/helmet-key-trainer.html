Perry Defensive Line Game 1 — Helmet Key Reaction Trainer

Helmet Key Reaction Trainer

This is the foundation game. The player must identify what the key’s helmet does at the snap and react fast. The goal is to remove hesitation and train immediate recognition.

Coach’s Rules

  • Don’t think. React.
  • Eyes on the helmet.
  • You are training instant recognition, not slow diagnosis.
  • Misses count. A corrected answer still counts as a miss on the run.
  • You must finish with a perfect run to earn the ticket.

Directions

  • Read the key cue.
  • Pick the correct helmet key immediately.
  • If you miss it, you will get coaching and must still choose the correct answer to move on.
  • Accuracy is based on first-click correctness.
