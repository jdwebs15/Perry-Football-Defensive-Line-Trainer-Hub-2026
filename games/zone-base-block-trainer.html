Start the Drill

This game trains the difference between base pressure and zone pressure when the outside hat and numbers come at you. The first steps stay the same. The reaction after contact changes based on the picture.

Steps Stay the Same

We may widen the pre-snap alignment 6” to 12” versus outside zone, but the first steps do not change.

Base Block

Shoot inside hand high center chest, outside hand to the shoulder, lock out, square to the LOS, and fight base pressure.

Zone Block

Impact with both hands, work the key vertical, set an edge with his body, and force the back to make a vertical cut.
