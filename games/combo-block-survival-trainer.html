Start the Drill

This game trains the survival rules against combo blocks. The offense wants vertical movement or wants the outside leverage player to overtake you and knock the inside leverage player up to the second level. Your job is to keep the combo intact on you, fight pressure, and maintain your gap.

First Step Stays the Same

The first step is always the same. Recognize the picture after the snap, but do not invent new footwork.

Hands Inside and Tight

As the hat and numbers of your key come at you, shoot your hands inside and tight just like any base block.

Keep the Combo Intact

Stay engaged with both hands on the base blocker, maintain your gap, and fight the vertical push.
